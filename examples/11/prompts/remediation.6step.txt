# REMEDIATION REASONING TEMPLATE
Use this template for manual remediation planning if LLM service is unavailable.

Step 1: Acknowledge Failure
- Describe what failed and why the verifier rejected the change.

Step 2: Clarify Goal
- Restate the desired behaviour now that the defect is known.

Step 3: Conflict Detection
- List conflicts between current implementation and requirements/history.

Step 4: Proposed Fix
- Detail the minimal, targeted change to resolve the issue.

Step 5: Risk Analysis
- Enumerate side effects and how to mitigate them.

Step 6: Validation Plan
- Specify tests and checks that will prove the fix works.
